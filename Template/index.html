What is Revenue Share?

  • Profit Distribution: When profit is generated, 70% of the profit goes to $TraderX token holders. The rest is distributed amongst our traders, into growing the treasury.
  • Proportional distribution: The distribution is based upon the amount of $TraderX tokens held by each holder. The more tokens held, the greater the proportional share of the profit distribution.
  • Payments: Payments will be made in Ethereum (ETH), rather than $TraderX tokens. This enables the continuing steady growth of the $TraderX ecosystem as well as reducing the inevitable ongoing sell pressure which may negatively affect the liquidity pool of $TraderX.
